Demo Program Terms and Conditions

Hello, and thank you for your interest in the RapidSpar Demo Program!

Please take a moment and read this through to familiarize yourself with the terms and conditions of participating in this program.

Qualification criteria:

In order to participate, you should:

Be an IT service provider in the US, Canada, UK, or Australia. Be in business for at least 3 years. Currently perform data recovery with software tools.

We may be open to working with organizations who fall outside of these criteria depending on the circumstances. If you’d like a demo unit, but don’t qualify, please contact us with some information about yourself and we will do our best to include you in this demo program.

Since RapidSpar is built to help IT generalists with occasional data recovery needs, this demo program is not intended for those who are already using professional data recovery tools. Data recovery professionals would be better served by our professional offerings (www.deepspar.com), rather than RapidSpar.

Cost:

The demo is free, but we will ask that you pay for shipping the demo device to the next participant after your trial period expires. In most cases, it would be domestic shipping. That’s all.

What we expect from you:

We created RapidSpar for IT service providers like you and your feedback is important to us. After you test the unit, we would like a short summary of your experience with it. It could be a testimonial, a review, or a social share. Anything you are comfortable with. We are looking for honest feedback on what the device can do, aimed at helping other IT service providers make the right decision on whether RapidSpar is a fit for their business.

How the program works:

Initially, demo units will be shipped from our Ottawa office to the first participants in line. You will receive an email with the tracking number and activation instructions a few days before your demo unit arrives. You will have a total of 7 business days to test the unit. After that, you will be asked to ship it directly to the next participant in line. You can use any postal service of your choice, but it must be insured and have a tracking number.

Limitations:

Demo units are fully functioning devices, however there is a lock in place that prevents their use without an internet connection. The demo license will expire in 7 business days, after which it will be impossible to use the device.

We hope you have a great experience testing out RapidSpar!

We are receiving a very large number of sign-ups for this demo program. The current estimated waiting time for approved applicants to receive demo units is 5 weeks.
